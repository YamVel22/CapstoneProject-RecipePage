Ingredients

  • 2 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk, warmed
  • 3 table spoons of butter cup butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup softend butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 113g of Creem cheese
  • 2 table spoons of softened butter

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine your dry ingredients- flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add your wet ingredients together- warm milk, melted butter, and egg. Mix well with the dry ingredients until a dough is formed.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth and elastic.
  4. Roll out the dough into a rectangle.
  5. Spread softened butter over the dough, then sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Roll the dough tightly into a log and cut into buns.
  7. Place buns in a greased baking dish and let rise until doubled in size.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 190°C for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade cinnamon buns!

Tips for making the best cinnamon rolls

For those aspiring to elevate their Cinnamon Bun game, here are some tried-and-true tips to ensure your creations are nothing short of spectacular. First and foremost, embrace patience during the dough rising process – allowing it ample time results in an airy, soft texture. When rolling out the dough, make sure it's evenly shaped for uniform swirls. For the filling, don't skimp on the cinnamon-sugar goodness; generously coat the dough to ensure each bite is a burst of flavor. When it comes to rolling, a tight spiral guarantees a beautiful swirl in every bun. To enhance the richness, consider adding a touch of vanilla extract or a hint of nutmeg to the filling. While baking, keep a watchful eye to achieve that perfect golden-brown hue. Once out of the oven, slather on a decadent cream cheese frosting while the buns are still warm – the contrast between the warm buns and cool frosting is simply divine. Finally, share the love – Cinnamon Buns are best enjoyed fresh, so indulge and spread the joy with those around you. Happy baking!
